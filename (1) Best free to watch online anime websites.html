1. Spirited away

Studio Ghibli became one of the biggest names in animation with the stunning and beloved Spirited Away. The fantasy film follows a young girl who becomes trapped in the spirit world. After her parents are transformed into pigs by a witch, she seeks a way to cure them and return to the human world. The film won the Oscar for Best Animated film and broke into the mainstream. Its stunning worldwide gross of $355,467,076 proved the studio was a major player in the industry and able to deliver financially successful films.

Facts about: Spirited away

  • Director: Hayao Miyazaki
